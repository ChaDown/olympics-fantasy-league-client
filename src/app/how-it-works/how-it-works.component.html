How Olympics Fantasy League Works

Create Your Team

Your team is made up of five countries from five different tiers. The tiers have been decided based on the medal table from Tokyo 2020, which you can view here to give you some hints.

The number beside each country is the number of participants they have at Paris 2024, for example Angola (20) means Team Angola are sending 20 athletes to Paris 2024.

Once you pick your countries, choose a team name, enter your email and password and click Register! And you're in! You should now be able to see your team on the leaderboard page.

Scoring

Points are awarded based on the number of medals your countries win.

  • Gold - 5 points
  • Silver - 3 points
  • Bronze - 1 point

But, there's a catch! Tier multipliers! To make it more competitive, there are points multipliers for each tier, with the lower tier teams having the potential to earn more points. The multipliers are as follows:

  • Tier 1 - No multiplier
  • Tier 2 - No multiplier
  • Tier 3 - 2x
  • Tier 4 - 3x
  • Tier 5 - 5x

Team Scoring Example

Team USA wins 10 gold, 5 silver and 3 bronze medals. Team USA is a Tier 1 country, so they earn (10 * 5) + (5 * 3) + (3 * 1) = 59 points.

Team Angola wins 1 gold, 1 silver and 0 bronze medals. Team Angola is a Tier 5 country, so they earn (1 * 5) + (2 * 3) + (0 * 1) = 11 * 5 (multiplier) = 55 points
